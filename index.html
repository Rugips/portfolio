About

Get to know the psychologist behind the immersive mental health projects.

I'm a general health psychologist trained at the Universitat de Barcelona, where I completed my bachelor's, master's, and postgraduate studies focused on empowering personal change. My work centers on translating clinical insights into practical tools that support people experiencing psychosis and other severe mental disorders.

I collaborate with interdisciplinary teams—researchers, clinicians, and technologists—to design studies, recruit participants, manage data, and deliver interventions. Recent roles include leading virtual reality research at Event Lab, contributing to psychosis treatment programs at Parc Sanitari Sant Joan de Déu, and building digital health content with Consultoria Setting.

  • Core focus: Clinical psychology, psychosis rehabilitation, VR research
  • Currently learning: Bayesian statistics, Unity XR, advanced data methods
  • Tools I rely on: EEG, SPSS, R, STATA, Ogama, Qualtrics, Unity
